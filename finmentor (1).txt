### **FinMentor Platform: Comprehensive Summary**

**Objective:**  
FinMentor is a platform designed to improve financial literacy among young adults in India by providing educational resources, financial management tools, and personalized mentoring services. It helps users learn about budgeting, investments, and wealth management, empowering them to make informed financial decisions.

---

### 🎯 **Core Features:**

1. **Educational Modules:**  
   - Structured content on budgeting, SIPs, SWPs, mutual funds, and tax planning.
   - Real-world examples and embedded video tutorials for better understanding.

2. **Financial Calculator:**  
   - Inputs: Monthly salary, expenses, and risk tolerance.  
   - Outputs:  
     - Recommended investment amount (20-30% of net income).  
     - Profit projections over time.  
     - Pie charts showing expense-investment breakdown.

3. **Personalized Dashboard:**  
   - **Expense Tracker:** Logs and categorizes daily/weekly expenses.  
   - **Investment Tracker:** Monitors ongoing investments and tracks progress toward financial goals.  
   - **Savings Goal Planner:** Auto-calculates required monthly savings to meet user-defined targets.

4. **AI-Powered Helpdesk:**  
   - AI chatbot for common financial queries.  
   - Option for 1-on-1 mentoring with financial advisors and tax experts.  

5. **Community Feedback Center:**  
   - Platform where users can share financial experiences and success stories.  
   - Peer learning and community engagement.  

6. **Subscription System:**  
   - Affordable subscription plans (Basic ₹99/month, Pro ₹299/month).  
   - Premium features accessible after subscription.
